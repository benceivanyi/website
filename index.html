Bence Iványi is a designer based in London, building brands and products with culture defining individuals and institutions. He’s currently leading the design of Autonomy, a digital art viewing and collecting app. Previous projects include work with galleries such as MoMA, The Barbican, Emalin, Des Bains, as well as digital art gallery Feral File. Having studied at Central Saint Martins, Bence developed the foundations of his practice in systems design and typography, paired with a concept–first approach that continues to inform his creative output.
